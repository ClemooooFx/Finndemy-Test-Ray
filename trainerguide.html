Finndemy User Guide - Trainers

Course Setup & Certification

Enabling Certificates and CPD Hours
  • Turn on certificate generation for your courses.
  • Set minimum completion requirements for students to earn certificates.
  • Specify CPD hours which reflect course duration and learning activities.
  • Courses can be aligned with industry and professional body accreditations.
How to Create a Course
  • Log in and go to the Instructor Dashboard.
  • Click on "Create New Course".
  • Enter your course title and description.
  • Choose and upload a featured image:
    • Select the image and click “Open”.
    • After upload, click “Select” at the bottom right.
  • Click “Save to Draft” at the top right to save your progress.

Earnings & Payment Details

Commission Structure & Payment
  • Trainers earn 20% commission of course sales revenue.
  • Payments are processed monthly by the 15th, provided minimum threshold is met.
  • Payment methods include M-PESA, PayPal, bank transfers, and more.
  • Track your earnings and payments with real-time dashboard analytics.

Account Management

How to Set Up Your Trainer Account
  • Profile Access: Log in and go to “Profile Details” to upload a professional headshot (min. 300x300px).
  • Navigate to “Instructor Dashboard” → “Settings”.
  • Trainer Profile:
    • Edit basic details and write a 200–300 word biography.
    • Add education history, achievements, and professional links.
    • Include website or portfolio links.
  • Banking & Payment: Add your PayPal email under "Paypal" settings.
  • Notifications: Enable alerts for enrollments, messages, and quiz completions.
  • Verification & Approval:
    • Submit ID, certificates, credentials, and work samples.
    • Background check takes 3–5 business days; you'll be updated via email.
    • Complete mandatory orientation modules and live onboarding after approval.
How to Register as a Trainer
  • Go to finndemy.com
  • Click on "Become an Instructor" below the search bar.
  • Fill out the registration form with:
    • First and last name
    • Username
    • Professional email address
    • Phone number
    • Country/Region
  • Create a secure password:
    • Minimum 8 characters
    • Include uppercase and lowercase letters
    • Add numbers and special characters
    • Avoid common or personal words
  • Agree to the privacy policy and click “Submit”
  • Check your inbox for a confirmation email after successful registration.
How to Log Out
  • Ensure you're logged in to your Finndemy account.
  • On the top right corner (below the support email), your username and a “Logout” icon will appear.
  • Click “Log Out”.
  • You will be logged out and redirected to the homepage: finndemy.com
How to Reset Your Password
  • Visit finndemy.com
  • Click on “Log in” at the top right of the page.
  • Enter your email and password. If the password is incorrect, click “Lost your Password”.
  • On the next page, enter the email used to sign up and click “Reset Password”.
  • A confirmation message will appear stating that a password reset email has been sent.
  • Check your email inbox for the most recent message from Finndemy.
  • Open the email and click the provided reset link.
  • Enter a new, strong password and click “Save”.
  • Your browser may offer to save the new password. Choose “Save” if the device is secure.
  • You will see a confirmation that your password has been reset successfully.
  • You can now log in using your new credentials.
