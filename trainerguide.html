Finndemy User Guide - Trainers

Course Setup & Certification

Enabling Certificates and CPD Hours
  • Turn on certificate generation for your courses.
  • Set minimum completion requirements for students to earn certificates.
  • Specify CPD hours which reflect course duration and learning activities.
  • Courses can be aligned with industry and professional body accreditations.

Earnings & Payment Details

Commission Structure & Payment
  • Trainers earn 20% commission of course sales revenue.
  • Payments are processed monthly by the 15th, provided minimum threshold is met.
  • Payment methods include M-PESA, PayPal, bank transfers, and more.
  • Track your earnings and payments with real-time dashboard analytics.

Account Management

How to Register as a Trainer
  • Go to finndemy.com
  • Click on "Become an Instructor" below the search bar.
  • Fill out the registration form with:
    • First and last name
    • Username
    • Professional email address
    • Phone number
    • Country/Region
  • Create a secure password:
    • Minimum 8 characters
    • Include uppercase and lowercase letters
    • Add numbers and special characters
    • Avoid common or personal words
  • Agree to the privacy policy and click “Submit”
  • Check your inbox for a confirmation email after successful registration.
How to Log Out
  • While logged in, look at the top right corner of the page under the support email.
  • Your username will be shown along with a “Logout” icon.
  • Click on “Log Out” to exit your account.
  • You will be redirected to the homepage: finndemy.com
