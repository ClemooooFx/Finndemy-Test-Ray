Finndemy User Guide - Admins

Getting Started

How to Register Your Organization on Finndemy
  • Visit finndemy.com
  • Click the Sign Up button and register using your email or Google account.
  • After registering, log in to access your dashboard.
  • Click on the "FINNDEMY PLANS" tab at the top menu.
  • Fill in the Business Registration Form:
    • First & Last Name
    • Work Email
    • Phone Number
    • Organization Name
    • Number of Seats
  • Click “Register Business” and wait for confirmation email.
How to Log In
  • Visit finndemy.com
  • Click “Log In” at the top right corner.
  • Enter your username and password.
  • Tick “Remember Me” if using a trusted device.
  • Click Login to access your dashboard.

Organization & Team Management

How to Create a Group for Your Organization
  • Log in as the admin who purchased the Finndemy Plan.
  • Go to Group Dashboard → Select your organization using the “Change Organization” dropdown.
  • Click “Manage Organization” → then “Add Team”.
  • Add a team either:
    • With Existing Team Leader — tick box, search and select leader, assign courses.
    • With New Team Leader — leave box unticked, enter leader info, assign courses.
  • If seats are unavailable, purchase more via the “Finndemy Plans” link in the footer.
How to Add Team Members
  • Go to the Group Dashboard and select the correct organization.
  • Under Team Name, click “Team Members”.
  • Two methods to add members:
    • Single Entry
      • Click “Add Team Member”
      • Fill in required fields (First Name, Username) and click Save.
      • To link existing users, check “Already exists?”, search and select user, then click Save.
    • Bulk Import
      • Click “Import List” → Download template → Fill and upload CSV → Click Save.
How to Buy Courses for Your Organization
  • Click Home on the top menu when logged in.
  • Scroll down and click “Finndemy Plans” in the green footer.
  • Select a plan based on organization size:
    • Individual Standard: KES 30,000/50,000
    • Business Starter: KES 390,000/510,000
    • Business Professional: KES 1,185,000/1,550,000
    • Business Enterprise: KES 1,950,000/2,550,000
    • Custom (501+ employees): Custom pricing
  • Click “Buy Now” → Select package → Add seats → Add to cart → Proceed to checkout.

Account Management

How to Reset Password
  • Go to finndemy.com
  • Click “Log In” → Click “Lost your Password” if login fails.
  • Enter your email and click “Reset Password”.
  • Check your inbox and follow the reset link.
  • Enter a new password and click Save.
  • You will see a confirmation message and can now log in.
How to Log Out
  • While logged in, go to the top right corner of the screen.
  • Click on your username and then click “Log Out”.
  • You will be redirected to the homepage.
