Finndemy User Guide - Admins

Getting Started

How to Register Your Organization on Finndemy
  • Visit finndemy.com
  • Click the Sign Up button and register using your email or Google account.
  • After registering, log in to access your dashboard.
  • Click on the "FINNDEMY PLANS" tab at the top menu.
  • You’ll be redirected to the Business Registration Form. Fill in:
    • First & Last Name
    • Work Email (same as sign-up)
    • Phone Number
    • Organization Name
    • Number of Seats (employees)
  • Click “Register Business” to submit the form.
  • Once submitted, a confirmation message will appear stating: "Your group is being processed. We'll email you when ready."
  • Wait for Finndemy's email confirmation once your group is approved and active.
How to Log In
  • Visit finndemy.com
  • Click “Log In” at the top right corner.
  • Enter your registered username and password.
  • (Optional) Tick the “Remember Me” box for trusted devices.
  • Click Login. You will be redirected to your Finndemy account dashboard.

Access Management & Reporting

Admins can assign courses, manage groups, and monitor employee learning progress:

  • Track progress and generate compliance reports.
  • Receive customized analytics dashboards based on plan level.
